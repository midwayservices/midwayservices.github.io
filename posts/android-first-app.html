Build Your First Android App

Android development is fun and rewarding. Let’s build a simple “Hello World” app.

Step 1: Install Android Studio

Download Android Studio from Google’s official site and install it.

Step 2: Create a New Project

Choose “Empty Activity” and give your app a name. Select Kotlin as the language.

Step 3: Run on Emulator

Press the green run button ▶️ and your app will open in the emulator with “Hello World”.
